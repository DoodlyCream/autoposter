Build & Chill Auto Poster Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

Build & Chill Auto Poster is a locally-run application. All data is stored on your own device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

2. API Credentials

API keys and access tokens entered into Build & Chill Auto Poster are stored locally in a SQLite database on your Mac. They are used solely to authenticate with the respective social media platform APIs on your behalf and are never shared with any third party.

3. Content

Content you upload (videos, images) is stored locally in the application's queue folder on your device. It is only transmitted to the social platform(s) you select at the time of posting.

4. TikTok API

When you authenticate with TikTok through Build & Chill Auto Poster, we receive an access token which is stored locally on your device. We use this token only to upload content on your behalf via the official TikTok Content Posting API. We do not access, store, or share your TikTok profile data beyond what is required for content publishing.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

Build & Chill Auto Poster does not sell, rent, or share any of your data with third parties. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or any external data collection services.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at dan@buildchill.com.
