Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Data We Collect

AutoPoster is a locally-run application. All data is stored on your own device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

2. API Credentials

API keys and access tokens entered into the application are stored locally in a SQLite database on your Mac. They are used solely to authenticate with the respective social media platform APIs on your behalf and are never shared with any third party.

3. Content

Content you upload (videos, images) is stored locally in the application's queue folder on your device. It is only transmitted to the social platform(s) you select at the time of posting.

4. TikTok API

When you authenticate with TikTok, we receive an access token which is stored locally on your device. We use this token only to upload content on your behalf via the official TikTok Content Posting API. We do not access, store, or share your TikTok profile data beyond what is required for content publishing.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any of your data with third parties. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or any external data collection services.
