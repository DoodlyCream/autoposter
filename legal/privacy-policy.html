Build & Chill Auto Poster Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

Build & Chill Auto Poster is a self-hosted scheduling tool. When you use the application, the following data is stored locally on the device running the software:

  • Your connected social media account nicknames and OAuth access tokens
  • Post content you upload (videos, images, captions)
  • Post schedule and publishing history

We do not operate external servers that collect or store your data. All data remains on the device where the application is installed.

2. API Credentials & OAuth Tokens

OAuth access tokens obtained through connecting your social media accounts are stored in an encrypted SQLite database on the host device. These tokens are used solely to publish content to the respective platforms on your behalf and are never transmitted to any third party.

3. Content You Upload

Videos and images you upload are stored in a local queue folder on the host device. Content is transmitted only to the social platform(s) you select at the time of scheduling. We do not store copies of your content on any external server.

4. TikTok Data Usage

When you connect your TikTok account through Build & Chill Auto Poster, we use the TikTok Content Posting API to publish videos and photo carousels on your behalf. We request only the minimum permissions required for content publishing:

  • video.upload / video.publish — to upload and post video content
  • photo.publish — to post photo carousels
  • user.info.stats — to display basic account information within the app

We do not access your TikTok inbox, followers list, or any data beyond what is explicitly listed above. Your TikTok access token is stored locally and is never shared with any third party.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

Build & Chill Auto Poster does not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties. The application does not use analytics services, tracking pixels, or any external data collection.

6. Data Deletion

You can remove a connected account at any time from the Settings page. This deletes the stored access token for that account. To remove all data, uninstall the application and delete the db/ folder from the install directory.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at dan@buildchill.com.
